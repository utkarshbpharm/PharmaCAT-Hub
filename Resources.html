4. Apps for On-the-Go Learning

  • Anki / Quizlet: Flashcards for vocab & formulas.
  • Pocket / Feedly: Save articles for VARC practice.
  • Spotify Podcasts: Listen to English comprehension podcasts daily.

5. Expert Tips for Using Resources

  • Pick one main book per section; avoid too many materials.
  • Use online content for clarity & revision, not theory building.
  • Make short notes and formula cards while studying.
  • Revise notes weekly and apply learning in mocks.
Summary: Books → Build Concepts • Online Platforms → Reinforce • Notes → Revise & Master
